how to use

+   2 basic modes

There are two basic modes of interactivity, controlled by the button in the upper left of the map.
In the 'zoom/pan' mode, the mouse is used to zoom and pan the map. 
In the 'info' mode, the mouse shows tooltips and information on map and theme elements on mouse over or click.

themes

The various themes of the visualization can be activated by links embedded within the story aside.

  adapt and customize

The button opens a tool bar to control and change the representation of the active theme.
The tool bar has various states. By clicking on the button on the left, you go through the various menus.

 share the customized map

You can share exactly what you see using the share dialog . After you have chosen a theme, may be changed some parameters of its representation and panned and zoomed the map to the area of your interest, you can use this dialog to:

-  get an URL

to reproduce the actual thematic map anywhere in a HTML5 capable browser.

-  reuse and embed

the map with the actual theme by the HTML code you find in the share dialog. You can insert this code snippet anywhere in your page.
